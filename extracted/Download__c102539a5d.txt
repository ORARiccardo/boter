Post-2030 energy and climate framework

Main points
raised and
positions
expressed:

• The representatives of Danish industry expressed their
favourable position regarding the inclusion of an
electrification target or key performance indicator in the
upcoming Electrification Action Plan and in the revised
Governance Regulation
• The representatives of Danish industry expressed their
favourable position on maintaining the existing target and
sub-target architecture on renewable energy and energy
efficiency in the post-2030 policy package
• The Commission provided a general update on the state of
play of the post-2030 framework policy package, foreseen
to be adopted by the end of the year

Conclusions:

The parts agreed to continue exchanging input and feedback
throughout the development of this legislative package
