09 July 2026

Location:

Videoconference

Name(s) and

Commission
representative(s):

Yvo Volman, Dir CNECT.G
With officials from CNECT.G1

Name(s) of the
interest
representative(s):

Connect Europe network of Data Protection Officers

Subject matter:

Data Union strategy, Digital Omnibus proposal, ePrivacy reform

Main points
raised and
positions
expressed:

Aim of the meeting was to discuss the Digital Simplification
Omnibus with data protection officers of the
telecommunications companies.
Points covered in the discussion:
- The EU’s Data Union strategy;
- Regulatory simplification by the Digital Omnibus and
additional simplification needs in relation to the ePrivacy
Directive;
- Digital and data sovereignty.

Conclusions:

Connect Europe DPOs welcomed the engagement from the
Commission on this topic and will provide a short technical
paper. There might be follow up meetings.
