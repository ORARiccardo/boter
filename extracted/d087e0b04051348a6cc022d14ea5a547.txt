Conclusions:

The representatives presented an overview of the
organisation and of the role of biomethane in the European
energy system. They emphasised the security and resilience
benefits of gas distribution infrastructure, noting that it
operates through an analogue system.
Furthermore, they expressed support for hybrid heating
solutions combining electricity and gas as an alternative to
electric heat pumps for heating and cooling applications.
The Commission representative welcomed the exchange and
outlined the Commission’s political objectives in the field of
energy, and provided a general update on the state of play
for the post-2030 framework policy package.
