Siemens S.A./N.V.

Subject matter:

Exchange of views on digitalisation of electricity grids.

Main points
raised and
positions
expressed:

Siemens briefly presented the company, namely the part that
develops smart infrastructures. They subsequently presented their
views on smart ‘output’ indicators that are able to measure the
overall grid performance vs. ‘input’ indicators that are focusing
on measuring the assets as inputs. Measuring outputs is essential.
DG ENER presented the current initiatives on digitalisation with
a focus on the Strategic Roadmap for Digitalisation and AI in
Energy. ENER also mentioned the upcoming legal proposal on
future proofing electricity bills.
Siemens flagged the need to have clear and harmonised rules to
access, manage and use energy-related data. Access to data is
underpinning any digitalisation efforts. Interoperability is key.
An additional point discussed was the human resources needed
for the energy transition. ENER mentioned the upcoming LIFE
project that will help DSOs to build capacity to digitalise grids.

Conclusions:

Siemens expressed their willingness to provide inputs to the
relevant Commission initiatives and decision-making processes
that would speed up the digitalisation of grids.
