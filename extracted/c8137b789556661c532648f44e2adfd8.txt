Conclusions:

•

ESMO presented their activities relating to AI
in oncology, in particular on minimum
requirements for AI-based biomarkers in
clinical practice and on safe and
accountable use of Large Language Models.

•

Participants discussed the need for
prospective clinical evidence for AI use in
oncology and the important role of clinicians,
as well as the need for specialised AI skills
for clinicians.

•

ESMO welcomed the Apply AI initiative on
the Network of AI-powered Advanced
Screening Centres and expressed their wish
to contribute.
