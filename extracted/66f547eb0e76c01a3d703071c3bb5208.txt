Main points

The following points were covered during the meeting:
-

Conclusions:

EU Age Verification solution and the role of age
verification providers within it
The upcoming AV Scheme to register solutions following
the technical specifications of the solution
Availability of age verification mechanisms for minors, for
example using the EUDI Wallet.
Standards that are applicable in the area of age assurance
The possible use Age Estimation as a form of age
assurance in the EU AV solution and how this is applied in
other jurisdictions.

The AVPA welcomed the further engagement from the
Commission on this topic. The Commission invited the age
assurance community to provide input to the Commission on age
assurance, given also the EU Age Verification solution. A follow
up meeting is to be arranged after the Summer.
