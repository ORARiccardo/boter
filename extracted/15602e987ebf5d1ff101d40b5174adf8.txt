EUROPEAN COMMISSION

MEETING MINUTES

Date:

10 July 2026

Location:

DG ENER, Brussels

Name(s) and
function(s) of the
Commission
representative(s):

Vincent Berrutto, Head of Unit, ENER.B4

Name(s) of the
interest
representative(s):

InnoEnergy

Subject matter:

Skills for AI in Energy and EU policy on digitalisation and use
of AI in the energy sector

Main points
raised and
positions
expressed:

InnoEnergy presented a new educational initiative on Advanced
energy systems and AI financed via the Digital Europe
Programme and raised interest for the Strategic Roadmap for
digitalisation and AI in the energy sector including sovereignty
and resilience aspects.
DG ENER gave an overview of the energy policy priorities and
presented the recently adopted Strategic Roadmap for
Digitalisation and AI in the Energy Sector. DG ENER also
referred to the Technology Sovereignty Package, including the
Cloud and AI Development Act. The ongoing work on the SET
Plan, including its annual conference in November, were also
mentioned.

Conclusions:

Both sides agreed to keep each other informed of their activities
in the field of AI and energy.
