Name(s) and

Thomas Skordas, DDG DG CONNECT
Dimitri Lorenzani, HoU DG CONNECT

Name(s) of the

representative(s):

General Catalyst

Subject matter:

Start-ups ecosystem and EU competitiveness

Main points
raised and
positions
expressed:

General Catalyst (GC) presented its mission and activities.
GC operates globally, but 80 % of its investments are in
Europe and it manages assets worth €50 billion.
The discussion focussed on the need for Europe to invest
significantly in computing infrastructure and frontier AI and
the available and forthcoming investment instrument to do so.
GC proposed to organise a meeting between the Commission
and GC Founders by the autumn and presented the AI
Champions initiative, scheduled for November 2026. The
programme will be shared with CNECT for further
consideration.

Conclusions:

The participants agreed to remain in contact, also in view of a
meeting with Founders and the AI Champions Initiative.
